## The Artisan's Hand

Our work is born from the hands of **15 master artisans** who have inherited their craft through direct lineage. We reject mass production in favor of devout creation.

- **Materials:** 24k Gold Leaf (Thanjavur divinity), Teak Wood (aged 50+ years), and Vegetable/Mineral dyes.
- **Process:** We prioritize Sampradaya protocol precision over aesthetic appeal. If a specific flower is mandated by scripture, no beautiful substitute is accepted.
